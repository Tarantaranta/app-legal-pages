Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

1. Introduction

MemoryRevive ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and share information when you use the MemoryRevive mobile application (the "App") on Android and other supported platforms.

MemoryRevive is an AI-powered photo restoration and photo animation application. The App allows users to restore old, blurry, damaged, low-quality, or black-and-white photos, and to animate still photos using artificial intelligence technologies.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Photos, Images, and Camera Access: The App may access photos that you select from your device gallery or capture using your device camera. We use these photos only when you choose to use restoration, enhancement, colorization, or animation features. Uploaded images are processed for the requested AI operation and are not used to personally identify you.
  • Camera Permission: On Android, the App may request camera permission if you choose to take a photo directly inside the App. Camera access is used only for capturing images that you choose to restore, enhance, or animate.
  • Device Information: We may collect technical information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, app version, language settings, approximate region, device identifiers, and diagnostic information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, including selected features, restoration or animation actions, credit usage, app interactions, error logs, and performance data.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases or subscribe to premium features, we may receive transaction-related information from Google Play Billing, Apple App Store, and RevenueCat. This may include purchase status, subscription status, product identifiers, credit pack purchases, renewal status, and entitlement information.
  • Account and Credit Data: We may store user identifiers, credit balance, subscription status, purchase history, restoration history, animation history, and app preferences through our backend systems.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and the information you provide in your message.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide AI photo restoration, enhancement, colorization, and animation features.
  • To process photos selected or captured by the user.
  • To save restored or animated results to your device when you choose to do so.
  • To manage subscriptions, premium access, credit packs, and credit balances.
  • To validate purchases and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • To refund or restore credits when an image operation fails.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To detect technical problems, crashes, bugs, or failed image processing requests.
  • To respond to user support requests.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, tax, security, and platform requirements.

4. Photo Processing and Storage

Photos selected or captured by the user are used only for the image processing features requested by the user. Depending on the selected feature, images may be temporarily uploaded to secure third-party AI processing services.

Processed images may be returned to the App and saved to your device if you choose to save them. We do not sell your photos. We do not use your photos for advertising. We do not intentionally use your uploaded photos to personally identify you.

Temporary processing files may be retained for a limited period by our service providers according to their technical and operational requirements. We aim to keep image processing temporary and limited to what is necessary to provide the requested feature.

5. Third-Party Services

MemoryRevive may use the following third-party services:

  • Replicate: Used for AI-based image restoration, enhancement, or related image processing features.
  • fal.ai: Used for AI-based photo animation or video generation features.
  • Supabase: Used for backend database services, user records, credit management, logs, and app-related data storage.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscriptions, entitlements, credit pack purchases, purchase validation, and premium access across Google Play Billing and Apple App Store.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process Android in-app purchases, subscriptions, renewals, and payment-related transactions.
  • Analytics and Diagnostics Services: We may use analytics, crash reporting, or diagnostic tools to understand app performance, identify errors, and improve the user experience.

These service providers may process data only as necessary to provide their services to the App. Their data handling practices are governed by their own privacy policies and service terms.

6. Data Sharing

We may share information in the following situations:

  • With AI Processing Providers: Photos may be temporarily shared with AI processing services when you request restoration, enhancement, colorization, or animation.
  • With Payment and Subscription Providers: Purchase and subscription information may be shared with Google Play Billing, Apple App Store, and RevenueCat to validate purchases and manage premium access.
  • With Backend and Cloud Service Providers: Account, credit, usage, and technical data may be stored or processed through secure backend services.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, court order, governmental request, or to protect our rights, users, security, or the integrity of the App.

We do not sell your personal information.

7. Data Retention

  • Photos and Images: Photos are processed temporarily for the requested AI operation. We aim to avoid retaining uploaded images longer than necessary for processing and delivery of the result.
  • Account and Credit Data: Account-related data, subscription status, purchase records, and credit balances may be retained as long as your account is active or as needed for legal, tax, fraud prevention, support, or operational purposes.
  • Usage and Diagnostic Data: Usage logs, error logs, and diagnostic information may be retained for app improvement, security, and troubleshooting.
  • Support Messages: Support communications may be retained as needed to respond to your request and maintain service records.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These measures may include encrypted data transmission, secure backend services, access controls, and limited data retention practices.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your data using commercially reasonable safeguards.
