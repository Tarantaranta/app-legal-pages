Privacy Policy for MemoryRevive

Effective date: April 20, 2025

1. Information Collection

MemoryRevive does not collect or store any personal user data. The app processes images locally on your device or via secure third-party services (Replicate API) for enhancement purposes only.

When you use our image restoration features, the following occurs:

  • Images are processed temporarily and securely
  • No permanent storage of your images on our servers
  • Processing is done through encrypted connections

2. Data Usage

The images you process through MemoryRevive are:

  • Used solely for providing the requested restoration services
  • Not used for training AI models or improving the service
  • Automatically deleted after processing is complete

3. Third-Party Services

We use Replicate API for image processing. Their privacy policy can be found at https://replicate.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage

Restored images are saved only to your device's gallery. We do not maintain copies of your original or processed images.

5. App Permissions

MemoryRevive requires the following permissions:

  • Camera access - for taking photos
  • Photo library access - for selecting and saving images
  • Internet access - for processing images via API
